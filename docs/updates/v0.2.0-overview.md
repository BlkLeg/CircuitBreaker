# Circuit Breaker v0.2.0 Overview

Welcome to the **v0.2.0** high-level overview. This release focuses on major architectural upgrades, bringing massive improvements to performance, enterprise-grade security, live interactions, and our next-generation discovery engine. We've optimized the stack from the ground up to ensure Circuit Breaker remains lightweight yet capable of handling complex environments.

Here is a breakdown of the user impact and new capabilities introduced in this release:

## Foundation & Performance

- **Caddy API Gateway**: We've replaced our routing tier with Caddy, giving you automatic HTTPS/TLS out of the box with zero configuration while drastically reducing RAM configuration overhead.
- **Analytics Engine**: Heavy, read-only analytics and catalog searches are now powered by **DuckDB**, ensuring the main application remains incredibly snappy even when querying large datasets.
- **Blazing Fast UX**: We've optimized the frontend bundle with aggressive code-splitting and lazy-loading. Complex pages (like Topology or Discovery) only load when needed, dropping the bundle size and making the initial load nearly instant.
- **Developer Experience**: Under the hood, a massive monorepo restructure has reduced the development cycle time by 50% for contributors, paving the way for faster feature iteration.

## Security & Enterprise Ready

- **Robust Authentication & Roles**: We've integrated a hardened authentication stack with JWT, session management, and a comprehensive Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) system supporting Admin, Editor, and Viewer roles.
- **API Rate Limiting**: Intelligent rate limiting now protects core endpoints from abuse, preventing service degradation during heavy automated usage or brute-force attempts.
- **Searchable Audit Logs**: A dedicated, fully searchable audit log system now tracks crucial system events (logins, configuration changes, scans) for compliance and operational transparency.
- **Local CVE & Vulnerability Insights**: Your scan results are now automatically correlated against a local, offline database of CVEs. You get immediate vulnerability insights without your infrastructure data ever leaving your network.

## Discovery Engine 2.0

- **Always-On Listener**: A new passive background listener uses `mDNS` and `SSDP` to instantly detect new devices the moment they broadcast on the network—no manual triggers required.
- **Deep-Dive Probing**: When a manual or scheduled scan is triggered, the engine now leverages Scapy and Masscan for aggressive TCP/IP probing, deep service banner grabbing, and highly accurate OS detection.
- **Smart Remediation**: Discovered services and banners are automatically cross-referenced with our new local CVE engine to instantly highlight susceptible targets in your environment.

## Real-time UX

- **Live Notifications & Status**: Powered by Server-Sent Events (SSE), you will now see real-time alerts, system logs, and scan progress seamlessly overlaid in the application without needing to refresh.
- **Interactive 3D Mapping**: WebSockets now drive our interactive topology and rack views. Adding, moving, or cabling a device happening in real-time, giving you a collaborative, live view of your infrastructure.
- **NATS Message Bus**: A new ultra-lightweight internal message broker manages data flow behind the scenes, ensuring the UI accurately reflects the true background state of your system with zero latency.
